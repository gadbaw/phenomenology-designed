Patterns at scale

Distant-reading views over 34763 meaning-units · 58 documents · 620 arcs. Model-coded — hypothesis-generating.

How to read these (the clean analytic layer). The corpus is pruned of non-case-writing (manuals, assessment, pure theory). Quantitative views use experiential moments only (locus≠none) and aggregate per document, then average — so one prolific book can't stand in for a tradition. Schools with fewer than 5 documents are dimmed and marked ⚠. Measurement reliability (model re-scoring the same passages): locus κ≈0.70 (clinician-vs-patient, the headline contrast, is the most stable; dyadic is the noisiest), outcome 92%, intensity MAE 0.10. Treat single-document schools and the dyadic band with caution.
